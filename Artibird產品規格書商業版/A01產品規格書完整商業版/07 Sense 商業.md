# 3. Product Commercial Overview

# 3. 產品商業總覽

## EN

Artibird Sense is the environmental sensing product family within the Artibird ecosystem. It provides environmental data and contextual signals that allow the system to understand changing conditions inside homes, classrooms, offices, campuses, commercial buildings, and selected outdoor or agricultural environments.

The Artibird Sense family may include temperature and humidity sensors, light sensors, occupancy or presence sensors, air-quality variants, door / window sensors, water-leak sensors, soil or irrigation-related sensors, and other specialized sensing variants depending on product roadmap and validation status. Each variant should be commercially described according to its actual sensor type, accuracy range, power profile, installation method, and validated operating conditions.

In a complete Artibird deployment, Sense works with Artibird Hub, Gate, ArtiOS, Switch, Panel, Act, Sync, Vision, OceanAI Cloud, and OceanAI Edge. A light sensor may help automate lighting; a temperature sensor may support climate control; occupancy data may influence room scenes; water or soil data may support infrastructure or irrigation workflows; and energy-related actions may be coordinated with Act and Sync.

Artibird Sense follows a local-first, cloud-enhanced model. Supported sensor readings, threshold events, and automation triggers can operate locally when required system components are present. Cloud services may enhance Sense with remote monitoring, alerts, historical reports, analytics, lifecycle management, and multi-site visibility.

Artibird Sense should not be presented as a universal sensor for all environments. Accuracy, range, sampling rate, battery life, environmental tolerance, installation method, calibration requirements, and automation behavior must be defined by product variant and validated under specified conditions.

## ZH-TW

Artibird Sense 是 Artibird 生態中的環境感知產品家族。它提供環境數據與上下文信號，使系統能夠理解住宅、教室、辦公室、校園、商業建築，以及部分戶外或農業環境中的變化條件。

Artibird Sense 家族可依產品路線與驗證狀態包含溫濕度感測器、光照感測器、人在或存在感測器、空氣品質版本、門窗感測器、漏水感測器、土壤或灌溉相關感測器，以及其他專用感測版本。每一個版本的商業描述都應依其實際感測類型、精度範圍、供電曲線、安裝方式與已驗證運作條件來定義。

在完整 Artibird 部署中，Sense 與 Artibird Hub、Gate、ArtiOS、Switch、Panel、Act、Sync、Vision、OceanAI Cloud 和 OceanAI Edge 協同。光照感測器可協助照明自動化；溫度感測器可支援空調控制；人在數據可影響房間場景；漏水或土壤數據可支援基礎設施或灌溉工作流；能源相關動作也可與 Act 和 Sync 協同。

Artibird Sense 採用本地優先、雲端增強模式。在所需系統元件具備的條件下，受支援感測讀數、閾值事件與自動化觸發可在本地運行。雲端服務可進一步為 Sense 增加遠端監測、警報、歷史報告、分析、生命週期管理與多站點可視化。

Artibird Sense 不應被表述為適用所有環境的通用感測器。精度、範圍、採樣頻率、電池壽命、環境耐受性、安裝方式、校準要求與自動化行為，必須依產品版本定義並在指定條件下完成驗證。

# 12. Commercial Claim Policy

# 12. 商業宣稱政策

## EN

All Artibird Sense commercial claims must remain consistent with the related engineering specification and validation results. Commercial materials must not use absolute claims such as “detects everything,” “always accurate,” “works in all environments,” “guaranteed energy savings,” “unlimited battery life,” or “universal automation trigger.”

Sense-related claims must distinguish between sensor variants, measurement types, accuracy ranges, reporting intervals, battery profiles, calibration requirements, installation conditions, and supported automation behavior. Environmental data should be presented as context for automation and monitoring, not as guaranteed outcome control unless validated.

Energy-saving, comfort-improvement, occupancy-detection, and environmental-accuracy claims must be supported by validated test conditions or expressed with cautious wording.

## ZH-TW

所有 Artibird Sense 商業宣稱必須與對應工程規格書與驗證結果保持一致。商業資料不得使用「偵測所有情況」「永遠準確」「適用所有環境」「保證節能」「無限制電池壽命」「通用自動化觸發器」等絕對化宣稱。

Sense 相關宣稱必須區分感測器版本、測量類型、精度範圍、上報間隔、電池曲線、校準要求、安裝條件與受支援自動化行為。環境數據應被表述為自動化與監測的上下文，而不是保證結果控制，除非已完成驗證。

節能、舒適度提升、人在偵測與環境精度相關宣稱，必須由已驗證測試條件支持，或使用謹慎表述。

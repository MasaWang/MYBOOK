## 1. Document Positioning

## 1. 文件定位

**EN**  
The **Artibird SKU & Model Naming Specification v1.0** is the official naming governance document for the Artibird product family. It defines how Artibird products are named, coded, versioned, packaged, regionally separated, commercially tracked, and prepared for certification, production, sales, distribution, and e-commerce listing.

This document is not a product catalog. It is an internal and operational control document used to connect product identity with hardware configuration, certification status, packaging, inventory, pricing, and commercial release.

**ZH-TW**  
**《Artibird SKU 與型號命名規範 v1.0》** 是 Artibird 產品家族的官方命名治理文件。它定義 Artibird 產品如何命名、編碼、區分版本、配置包裝、劃分區域、追蹤商業狀態，並為認證、生產、銷售、渠道與電商上架做好準備。

本文件不是產品型錄，而是一份內部與運營管控文件，用於將產品身份與硬體配置、認證狀態、包裝、庫存、價格與商業發布連接起來。

---

## 2. Document Purpose

## 2. 文件目的

**EN**  
The purpose of this document is to prevent product identity confusion across engineering, certification, packaging, sales, channel communication, Amazon listing, inventory management, and future product expansion.

A clear SKU and model naming system allows Artibird products to remain traceable from concept to prototype, from validation to certification, and from packaging to commercial release.

**ZH-TW**  
本文件的目的，是防止工程、認證、包裝、銷售、渠道溝通、Amazon 上架、庫存管理與未來產品擴展過程中的產品身份混亂。

清晰的 SKU 與型號命名系統，可使 Artibird 產品從概念、原型、驗證、認證，到包裝與商業發布的全流程保持可追蹤。

## Chapter 2 — Naming Principles

## 第二章｜命名原則

### Purpose

### 目的

**EN**  
Define the principles that govern all Artibird customer-facing names, internal model names, SKU codes, package names, and certification model references.

**ZH-TW**  
定義所有 Artibird 面向客戶名稱、內部型號、SKU 代碼、包裝名稱與認證型號引用的命名原則。

### Core Principles

### 核心原則

|Principle|Description|
|---|---|
|Clear|Names must be easy to understand|
|Stable|Product line codes should not change frequently|
|Traceable|Each SKU must connect to hardware, firmware, region, and package|
|Non-overlapping|Two SKUs should not describe the same configuration unless intentionally separated|
|Region-aware|Market differences must be visible where needed|
|Certification-aware|Certification claims must not be implied by SKU unless approved|
|Commercially usable|Names must work for catalog, website, packaging, Amazon, channel, and inventory|
|Scalable|Naming must support future generations and variants|

|原則|說明|
|---|---|
|清晰|名稱必須容易理解|
|穩定|產品線代碼不應頻繁更改|
|可追蹤|每個 SKU 必須能連接硬體、韌體、區域與包裝|
|不重疊|兩個 SKU 不應描述同一配置，除非因渠道或包裝刻意區分|
|區域感知|必要時必須體現市場差異|
|認證感知|除非已核准，SKU 不應暗示認證狀態|
|可商業使用|名稱必須適用於型錄、網站、包裝、Amazon、渠道與庫存|
|可擴展|命名必須支援未來世代與版本擴展|

## Chapter 14 — SKU Governance Rules

## 第十四章｜SKU 治理規則

### Purpose

### 目的

**EN**  
Define governance rules for creating, modifying, approving, retiring, and reusing SKU codes.

**ZH-TW**  
定義 SKU 建立、修改、核准、退市與重用的治理規則。

### Governance Rules

### 治理規則

|Rule|Requirement|
|---|---|
|One SKU, one configuration|Each SKU must represent one defined product configuration|
|No unsupported codes|Do not use codes for unimplemented functions|
|No certification implication|SKU must not imply certification unless approved|
|No duplicate SKU meaning|Duplicate configurations must be avoided|
|Version traceability|SKU must connect to hardware, firmware, and package version|
|Change approval|SKU changes require review|
|Retirement control|Deprecated SKUs must be marked inactive|
|Commercial consistency|SKU must match catalog, packaging, Amazon, and sales documents|

|規則|要求|
|---|---|
|一個 SKU 對應一種配置|每個 SKU 必須代表一種已定義產品配置|

## F.1 Purpose

## F.1 目的

## EN

This appendix defines the Claim Boundary Reference for the OceanAI System Architecture Specification. The purpose of this appendix is to provide a structured reference for how engineering, commercial, website, sales, channel, deployment, certification, AI, privacy, security, capacity, reliability, and energy-related claims should be controlled.

Claim boundaries ensure that OceanAI is described according to its actual architecture, implementation status, validation evidence, version condition, deployment condition, and approved language.

A claim boundary does not weaken the system narrative. It protects the credibility of the architecture by ensuring that every external or internal statement remains accurate, evidence-based, and aligned with system reality.

## ZH-TW

本附錄定義《OceanAI 系統架構規格書》的宣稱邊界參考。此附錄的目的，是為工程、商業、網站、銷售、渠道、部署、認證、AI、隱私、安全、容量、可靠性與能源相關宣稱如何被管控提供結構化參考。

宣稱邊界確保 OceanAI 依據實際架構、實作狀態、驗證證據、版本條件、部署條件與已核准語言被描述。

宣稱邊界不會削弱系統敘事。它保護架構可信度，確保每一項對外或內部表述都保持準確、基於證據，並與系統現實一致。

## F.2 Claim Boundary Principles

## F.2 宣稱邊界原則

## EN

OceanAI claims must be evidence-based, condition-bound, version-bound, architecture-aligned, and commercially responsible.

A claim should not be used merely because it is technically possible, conceptually correct, visually impressive, commercially attractive, or aligned with future roadmap direction.

Every claim must answer five questions: what is being claimed, under what conditions, for which components, supported by what evidence, and approved for what usage.

## ZH-TW

OceanAI 的宣稱必須基於證據、綁定條件、綁定版本、與架構對齊，並具備商業責任。

某項宣稱不應僅因為技術上可行、概念上正確、視覺上有吸引力、商業上有吸引力，或符合未來路線圖方向，就被使用。

每一項宣稱都必須回答五個問題：宣稱什麼、在什麼條件下、適用於哪些組件、由什麼證據支持，以及被核准用於什麼場景。

---

## English Table

|Claim Boundary Principle|Description|
|---|---|
|Evidence-based|Claims must be supported by validation evidence, implementation records, or approved architecture definitions|
|Condition-bound|Claims must state the conditions under which they apply|
|Version-bound|Claims must be tied to supported hardware, firmware, software, cloud, Edge, runtime, or document versions|
|Architecture-aligned|Claims must align with approved architecture responsibilities and boundaries|
|Capability-specific|Claims must describe a specific supported capability rather than broad universal behavior|
|No overstatement|Claims must avoid absolute, unlimited, guaranteed, or universal language unless formally proven|
|Claim traceability|Claims should link to validation evidence, architecture sections, and approval records|
|Commercial control|Customer-facing language must be derived from validated engineering language|
|Certification control|Certification-related language must only be used when formally supported|
|Roadmap separation|Future plans must not be presented as current validated capabilities|

## 中文表

|宣稱邊界原則|說明|
|---|---|
|基於證據|宣稱必須由驗證證據、實作記錄或已核准架構定義支持|
|綁定條件|宣稱必須說明其適用條件|
|綁定版本|宣稱必須與受支援硬體、韌體、軟體、雲端、Edge、Runtime 或文件版本綁定|
|架構對齊|宣稱必須與已核准架構責任與邊界一致|
|能力具體|宣稱必須描述具體受支援能力，而不是廣泛通用行為|
|不過度表述|除非正式證明，宣稱必須避免絕對、無限制、保證或通用語言|
|宣稱可追溯|宣稱應連接至驗證證據、架構章節與核准記錄|
|商業管控|面向客戶語言必須由已驗證工程語言轉化而來|
|認證管控|認證相關語言僅可在正式證據支持下使用|
|路線圖分離|未來規劃不得被描述為當前已驗證能力|

## F.3 宣稱分類模型

## EN

The Claim Classification Model defines the major claim categories used in OceanAI. Each category requires different supporting evidence and different boundary language.

A claim may belong to more than one category. For example, “local AI-assisted visual alert” may involve Edge, AI, Vision, privacy, runtime, reliability, and deployment claim boundaries.

宣稱分類模型定義 OceanAI 中使用的主要宣稱類別。每一類宣稱都需要不同支持證據與不同邊界語言。

## F.4 Engineering Claim Boundary

## F.4 工程宣稱邊界

## EN

Engineering claims describe technical capability, behavior, performance, reliability, security, privacy, architecture, or compatibility. They must be precise, testable, and linked to implementation status.

Engineering claims should not use commercial simplification unless the technical boundary remains clear.

Every engineering claim should include related architecture section, affected component, version condition, test condition, evidence reference, and boundary statement.

## ZH-TW

工程宣稱描述技術能力、行為、性能、可靠性、安全、隱私、架構或相容性。它們必須精確、可測試，並連接至實作狀態。

工程宣稱不應使用過度商業化簡化語言，除非技術邊界仍保持清楚。

每項工程宣稱應包含關聯架構章節、受影響組件、版本條件、測試條件、證據參考與邊界聲明。

---

|Engineering Claim Field|Required Content|
|---|---|
|Claim statement|Exact technical statement|
|Related architecture|Chapter, appendix, matrix, or model supporting the claim|
|Related component|Product, service, layer, runtime function, or deployment|
|Implementation status|Implemented, partially implemented, prototype, planned, deprecated, unsupported, or retired|
|Version condition|Hardware, firmware, software, cloud, Edge, runtime, or model version|
|Test condition|Network, load, environment, deployment, or runtime condition|
|Evidence reference|Test report, log, measurement, validation record, certification evidence, or audit record|
|Boundary statement|Conditions under which the claim applies|
|Approval status|Draft, reviewed, approved, final, superseded, rejected, or archived|

|實作狀態|已實作、部分實作、原型、規劃中、退役中、不支援或已退役|

## F.5 Commercial Claim Boundary

## F.5 商業宣稱邊界

## EN

Commercial claims translate validated engineering capability into customer-facing, sales-facing, channel-facing, or website-facing language. Commercial language may be simpler than engineering language, but it must not expand the claim beyond the evidence.

Commercial claims must avoid universal compatibility, unlimited capacity, guaranteed outcomes, perfect AI performance, perfect security, absolute privacy, always-on behavior, or certification language unless formally supported.

Commercial claims should be reviewed against engineering evidence before publication.

## ZH-TW

商業宣稱將已驗證工程能力轉化為面向客戶、銷售、渠道或網站的語言。商業語言可以比工程語言更簡潔，但不得將宣稱擴展至證據之外。

除非具備正式支持，商業宣稱必須避免通用相容、無限制容量、保證結果、完美 AI 性能、完美安全、絕對隱私、永遠在線行為或認證語言。

商業宣稱在發布前應對照工程證據進行審查。

---

|Restricted Commercial Claim|Preferred Boundary Language|
|---|---|
|Works with everything|Works with supported and validated devices or components|
|Controls all home devices|Controls supported devices and appliances under defined compatibility conditions|
|Unlimited devices|Supports validated capacity under defined device, network, and runtime conditions|
|Always works offline|Supports defined local functions when required local components are present|
|Fully autonomous AI|Provides AI-assisted behavior under defined governance and validation boundaries|
|Perfect detection|Supports defined detection or interpretation under validated conditions|
|Guaranteed energy savings|Supports energy-aware workflows where validated|
|Completely secure|Designed with implemented and validated security controls|
|Fully private|Supports privacy-aware data handling under defined settings|
|No data ever leaves home|Specific data remains local where configured and supported|
|Certified safe|Use only after certification or listing is complete and approved|
|Never fails|Supports defined reliability, degraded operation, and recovery behavior|

|---|---|
|支援所有設備|支援受支援且已驗證設備或組件|
|控制所有家庭設備|在明確相容條件下控制受支援設備與家電|
|無限制設備|在明確設備、網路與 Runtime 條件下支援已驗證容量|
|永遠離線可用|當所需本地組件具備時，支援已定義本地功能|
|完全自主 AI|在已定義治理與驗證邊界下提供 AI 輔助行為|
|完美偵測|在已驗證條件下支援已定義偵測或解讀|

|已認證安全|僅在認證或 Listed 完成並核准後使用|
|永不故障|支援已定義可靠性、降級運作與恢復行為|

## F.6 Architecture Claim Boundary

## F.6 架構宣稱邊界

## EN

Architecture claims describe the identity and structure of OceanAI as a system-level architecture. These claims may describe layer responsibilities, coordinated intelligence, local-cloud-edge cooperation, runtime behavior, governance, security, privacy, deployment, and validation framework.

Architecture claims must not imply that every product, deployment, or service already implements every architecture capability.

An architecture claim may define the intended system structure, but implementation status and validated capability must be confirmed separately.

## ZH-TW

架構宣稱描述 OceanAI 作為系統級架構的身份與結構。這些宣稱可描述層級責任、協同智慧、本地—雲端—Edge 協作、Runtime 行為、治理、安全、隱私、部署與驗證框架。

架構宣稱不得暗示每一個產品、部署或服務都已實作所有架構能力。

架構宣稱可以定義預期系統結構，但實作狀態與已驗證能力必須另外確認。

---

## English Table

|Architecture Claim Type|Boundary Requirement|
|---|---|
|OceanAI as system architecture|May define system-level architecture identity, not product-specific readiness|
|Coordinated intelligent environment|Must describe coordination under supported components and runtime conditions|
|Local-cloud-edge coordination|Must define responsibility separation and dependency boundaries|
|Runtime architecture|Must distinguish architecture design from implemented runtime behavior|
|Governance architecture|Must define control rules without implying all controls are fully implemented|
|Security architecture|Must distinguish design controls from validated security effectiveness|
|Privacy architecture|Must distinguish privacy-aware design from legal or regulatory compliance|
|Deployment architecture|Must distinguish reference deployment patterns from validated site deployment|
|Validation framework|Must define evidence requirements, not substitute for actual test evidence|

## 中文表

|架構宣稱類型|邊界要求|
|---|---|
|OceanAI 作為系統架構|可定義系統級架構身份，但不代表特定產品就緒|
|協同智慧空間|必須描述受支援組件與 Runtime 條件下的協同|
|本地—雲端—Edge 協同|必須定義責任分離與依賴邊界|
|Runtime 架構|必須區分架構設計與已實作 Runtime 行為|
|治理架構|必須定義控制規則，但不暗示所有控制均已完整實作|
|安全架構|必須區分設計控制與已驗證安全有效性|
|隱私架構|必須區分隱私導向設計與法律或法規合規|
|部署架構|必須區分參考部署模式與已驗證站點部署|
|驗證框架|必須定義證據要求，但不取代實際測試證據|

## F.7 Local-First Claim Boundary

## F.7 本地優先宣稱邊界

## EN

Local-first claims describe functions that can operate locally when required local components are present. These claims must clearly define which functions work locally, which components are required, which functions degrade without cloud, and which functions still require cloud services.

Local-first does not mean all functions always work without Internet, cloud, account services, or synchronization.

Local-first claims must be tested under defined offline and degraded conditions.

## ZH-TW

本地優先宣稱描述當所需本地組件具備時，可在本地運作的功能。這些宣稱必須清楚定義哪些功能可本地運作、需要哪些組件、哪些功能在無雲端時降級，以及哪些功能仍需要雲端服務。

本地優先不表示所有功能永遠可在無 Internet、無雲端、無帳號服務或無同步條件下運作。

本地優先宣稱必須在明確離線與降級條件下完成測試。

---

## English Table

|Restricted Claim|Preferred Boundary Language|
|---|---|
|OceanAI works fully offline|Supported local functions continue when required local components are present|
|No Internet is ever needed|Certain local functions may operate without Internet; remote and cloud services require connectivity|
|Cloud is optional for everything|Cloud is optional for defined local behaviors, but required for remote access, account, lifecycle, and multi-site features|
|All automations work offline|Supported local automations operate when required local runtime, devices, and inputs are available|
|All scenes work offline|Supported local scenes operate when configured and required devices are reachable|
|App always works without Internet|App local access works under defined local network and discovery conditions|
|Offline equals no service loss|Offline operation may involve reduced remote, history, alert, sync, or lifecycle capability|
|Local-first guarantees reliability|Local-first improves continuity for supported functions but does not eliminate all failure modes|

## 中文表

|限制宣稱|建議邊界語言|
|---|---|
|OceanAI 完全離線可用|當所需本地組件具備時，受支援本地功能可繼續|
|永遠不需要 Internet|某些本地功能可無 Internet 運作；遠端與雲端服務需要連接|
|所有功能都不需要雲端|雲端對已定義本地行為可選，但遠端存取、帳號、生命週期與多站點功能需要雲端|
|所有自動化都可離線運作|當所需本地 Runtime、設備與輸入可用時，受支援本地自動化可運作|
|所有場景都可離線運作|在已配置且所需設備可達條件下，受支援本地場景可運作|
|App 永遠可無 Internet 使用|App 本地存取在已定義本地網路與發現條件下運作|
|離線等於沒有服務損失|離線運作可能導致遠端、歷史、警報、同步或生命週期能力降低|
|本地優先保證可靠性|本地優先提升受支援功能延續性，但不消除所有故障模式|

## F.8 Edge and AI Claim Boundary

## F.8 Edge 與 AI 宣稱邊界

## EN

Edge and AI claims describe AI-assisted interpretation, detection, recommendation, optimization, visual understanding, sensor fusion, or local inference. These claims must distinguish between interpretation, recommendation, alert, optimization support, and validated control input.

AI-assisted does not mean fully autonomous. Edge intelligence does not mean unrestricted authority over physical action.

Any AI output that affects physical execution must pass through runtime governance, permission, priority, safety, validation, and claim approval.

## ZH-TW

Edge 與 AI 宣稱描述 AI 輔助解讀、偵測、建議、優化、視覺理解、感測融合或本地推理。這些宣稱必須區分解讀、建議、警報、優化支持與已驗證控制輸入。

AI 輔助不等於完全自主。Edge 智慧不等於對物理動作具有不受限制權威。

任何影響物理執行的 AI 輸出，都必須經過 Runtime 治理、權限、優先級、安全、驗證與宣稱核准。

---

|Restricted Claim|Preferred Boundary Language|
|---|---|
|Fully autonomous AI system|AI-assisted coordinated system under defined governance and validation boundaries|
|AI understands everything in the room|AI-assisted interpretation of supported visual, sensor, or runtime context under validated conditions|
|Perfect visual detection|Supports defined visual event detection under tested conditions|
|AI makes decisions automatically|AI may provide recommendations or bounded inputs; runtime rules govern execution|
|Edge replaces cloud and runtime|Edge provides local AI-assisted interpretation and does not replace runtime or cloud responsibilities|
|AI guarantees safety|AI may support awareness or recommendation; safety behavior requires validated runtime and device controls|
|AI guarantees energy savings|AI may support energy-aware recommendations or optimization where validated|
|AI controls devices directly|AI output may influence action only through validated runtime governance|
|Local AI means no data risk|Local AI can reduce cloud transfer for supported data, but privacy controls and validation are still required|

## 中文表

|限制宣稱|建議邊界語言|
|---|---|
|完全自主 AI 系統|在已定義治理與驗證邊界下的 AI 輔助協同系統|
|AI 理解房間中的一切|在已驗證條件下，AI 輔助解讀受支援視覺、感測或 Runtime 上下文|
|完美視覺偵測|在已測條件下支援已定義視覺事件偵測|
|AI 自動做決策|AI 可提供建議或受邊界約束輸入；執行由 Runtime 規則治理|

|AI 保證節能|AI 可在已驗證條件下支持能源感知建議或優化|
|AI 直接控制設備|AI 輸出僅可透過已驗證 Runtime 治理影響動作|
|本地 AI 表示沒有數據風險|本地 AI 可降低受支援數據的雲端傳輸，但仍需要隱私控制與驗證|

## F.19 Claim Boundary

## F.19 宣稱邊界本身的邊界

## EN

This Claim Boundary Reference defines how claims should be controlled. It does not by itself approve any specific claim, validate any capability, certify any product, or confirm legal compliance.

A claim boundary is a governance reference. Actual claim approval requires evidence, review, and authorization.

Therefore, this appendix must be used together with architecture definitions, product specifications, validation records, certification documents, privacy policies, security reviews, legal reviews where applicable, and approved commercial language.

## ZH-TW

本宣稱邊界參考定義宣稱應如何被管控。它本身不核准任何特定宣稱、不驗證任何能力、不認證任何產品，也不確認法律合規。

宣稱邊界是一種治理參考。實際宣稱核准需要證據、審查與授權。

因此，本附錄必須與架構定義、產品規格書、驗證記錄、認證文件、隱私政策、安全審查、適用時的法律審查，以及已核准商業語言一起使用。

## 1. Purpose

## 1. 目的

## EN

This checklist is used to perform the final review of the **OceanAI System Architecture Specification v1.1** before it is marked as **v1.1-Final**.

The purpose of this review is to confirm that the document is structurally complete, architecturally consistent, terminology-aligned, runtime-coherent, data-model-aligned, validation-ready, claim-boundary-controlled, and suitable to serve as the architecture-level reference for the OceanAI / Artibird coordinated intelligent environment system.

This checklist does not validate implementation, certification, product readiness, or commercial availability. It validates the document’s readiness as a formal architecture specification.

# 3. Product Commercial Overview

# 3. 產品商業總覽

## EN

Artibird Vision is the AI vision and visual context product family within the Artibird ecosystem. It provides visual signals, security awareness, and contextual information that allow intelligent environments to understand what is happening in a space, rather than relying only on device states or simple sensor readings.

The Artibird Vision family may include indoor cameras, outdoor cameras, room cameras, classroom cameras, entry monitoring cameras, AI-assisted vision nodes, and edge-connected visual perception variants depending on product roadmap and validation status. Each variant should be commercially described according to its camera type, resolution, field of view, night-vision capability, storage method, network mode, privacy features, AI-processing location, and validated operating conditions.

In a complete Artibird deployment, Vision works with Artibird Hub, Gate, ArtiOS, Sense, Act, Switch, Panel, Sync, OceanAI Cloud, and OceanAI Edge. A visual event may trigger a notification; a room-state signal may support automation; a detected presence condition may work with lighting or HVAC logic; a security event may trigger Act, Panel, or Cloud workflows; and OceanAI Edge may assist with local visual understanding where deployed.

Artibird Vision follows a privacy-aware, local-first, cloud-enhanced model. Supported local visual events and recording workflows may operate within the local environment when required system components are present. Cloud services may enhance Vision with remote viewing, alerts, account-level access, historical event browsing, lifecycle management, and multi-site visibility. OceanAI Edge may further support local AI-assisted analysis and response when deployed.

Artibird Vision should not be presented as a universal AI camera. Detection accuracy, recognition behavior, field coverage, night performance, recording reliability, privacy behavior, storage limits, network performance, and AI capability must be defined by product variant and validated under specified conditions.

## ZH-TW

Artibird Vision 是 Artibird 生態中的 AI 視覺與視覺上下文產品家族。它提供視覺信號、安全感知與上下文資訊，使智慧環境能夠理解空間中正在發生什麼，而不只是依賴設備狀態或簡單感測讀數。

Artibird Vision 家族可依產品路線與驗證狀態包含室內攝像機、室外攝像機、房間攝像機、教室攝像機、入口監測攝像機、AI 輔助視覺節點，以及接入 Edge 的視覺感知版本。每一個版本的商業描述都應依其攝像頭類型、解析度、視野角、夜視能力、儲存方式、網路模式、隱私功能、AI 處理位置與已驗證運作條件來定義。

在完整 Artibird 部署中，Vision 與 Artibird Hub、Gate、ArtiOS、Sense、Act、Switch、Panel、Sync、OceanAI Cloud 和 OceanAI Edge 協同。視覺事件可觸發通知；房間狀態信號可支援自動化；偵測到的存在條件可與照明或 HVAC 邏輯配合；安全事件可觸發 Act、Panel 或 Cloud 工作流；部署 OceanAI Edge 時，Edge 可進一步支援本地視覺理解與回應。

Artibird Vision 採用注重隱私、本地優先、雲端增強模式。在所需系統元件具備的條件下，受支援本地視覺事件與錄影工作流可在本地環境中運作。雲端服務可進一步為 Vision 增加遠端查看、警報、帳號級存取、歷史事件瀏覽、生命週期管理與多站點可視化。部署 OceanAI Edge 時，還可支援本地 AI 輔助分析與回應。

Artibird Vision 不應被表述為通用 AI 攝像機。偵測精度、識別行為、視野覆蓋、夜間表現、錄影可靠性、隱私行為、儲存限制、網路性能與 AI 能力，必須依產品版本定義並在指定條件下完成驗證。

# 12. Commercial Claim Policy

# 12. 商業宣稱政策

## EN

All Artibird Vision commercial claims must remain consistent with the related engineering specification, privacy policy, data policy, and validation results. Commercial materials must not use absolute claims such as “detects everything,” “perfect AI recognition,” “zero false alarms,” “works in all lighting conditions,” “unlimited recording,” “fully private by default,” or “guaranteed security.”

Vision-related claims must distinguish between camera variants, recording modes, AI-processing locations, event types, detection methods, field of view, lighting conditions, storage limits, network requirements, and privacy behavior. AI-related claims must be based on validated detection categories, test conditions, model version, and known limitations.

Security, privacy, remote viewing, and recording claims must be especially cautious. Any claim about privacy protection, local processing, cloud storage, data retention, user permissions, or remote access must match actual product behavior and published policies.

## ZH-TW

所有 Artibird Vision 商業宣稱必須與對應工程規格書、隱私政策、數據政策與驗證結果保持一致。商業資料不得使用「偵測所有情況」「完美 AI 識別」「零誤報」「適用所有光照條件」「無限制錄影」「預設完全隱私」「保證安全」等絕對化宣稱。

Vision 相關宣稱必須區分攝像頭版本、錄影模式、AI 處理位置、事件類型、偵測方法、視野角、光照條件、儲存限制、網路要求與隱私行為。AI 相關宣稱必須基於已驗證偵測類別、測試條件、模型版本與已知限制。

安全、隱私、遠端查看與錄影相關宣稱必須尤其謹慎。任何關於隱私保護、本地處理、雲端儲存、數據保留、用戶權限或遠端存取的宣稱，都必須與實際產品行為與已發布政策一致。

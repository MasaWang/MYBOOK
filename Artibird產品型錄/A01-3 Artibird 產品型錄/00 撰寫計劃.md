## 1. Document Positioning

## 1. 文件定位

## EN

**Artibird Product Catalog v1.0** is the official product-line presentation document for the Artibird product layer within the OceanAI coordinated intelligent environment system.

It is not an engineering specification, not a system specification, and not a pure marketing brochure. It is a system-based product catalog that explains what Artibird products are, how they are positioned, how they participate in OceanAI, and how they can be combined into residential, classroom, campus, building, energy, vision-aware, agriculture, and infrastructure scenarios.

## ZH-TW

《**Artibird 產品型錄 v1.0**》是 OceanAI 協同智慧空間系統中 Artibird 產品層的正式產品線展示文件。

它不是工程規格書，不是系統規格書，也不是單純行銷冊。它是一份**系統型產品型錄**，用於說明 Artibird 產品是什麼、如何定位、如何參與 OceanAI，以及如何組合成住宅、教室、校園、建築、能源、視覺感知、農業與基礎設施場景方案。

# 3. Catalog Writing Principle

# 3. 型錄撰寫原則

## EN

The Artibird Product Catalog should follow five principles:

|Principle|Description|
|---|---|
|System-first|Products must be explained as part of the OceanAI system, not isolated devices|
|Product-clear|Each product must have clear name, role, function, and use case|
|Customer-readable|Language should be easier than engineering specifications|
|Technically credible|Claims must remain accurate and evidence-aware|
|Sales-ready|Content should support websites, sales decks, channel partners, and product pages|

## ZH-TW

Artibird 產品型錄應遵循五項原則：

|原則|說明|
|---|---|
|系統優先|產品必須被說明為 OceanAI 系統的一部分，而不是孤立設備|
|產品清晰|每個產品必須具備清楚名稱、角色、功能與應用場景|
|客戶可讀|語言應比工程規格書更易理解|

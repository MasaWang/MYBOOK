## 15.1 Product Combination Positioning

## 15.1 產品組合定位

**EN**  
The Energy & Facility Management Product Combination defines how Artibird products can support energy monitoring, energy-aware automation, facility control, building management, and operational efficiency across residential, educational, office, and commercial spaces.

It integrates Hub, Gate, Act / Act-E, Sense, Sync, Panel, App, Cloud, and Edge into coordinated workflows that provide energy visibility, load control, environmental monitoring, and actionable insights.

**ZH-TW**  
能源與設施管理產品組合定義 Artibird 產品如何支援能源監測、能源感知自動化、設施控制、建築管理與運行效率，適用於住宅、教育、辦公與商業空間。

它整合 Hub、Gate、Act / Act-E、Sense、Sync、Panel、App、Cloud 與 Edge，形成協同工作流，提供能源可視化、負載控制、環境監測與可執行洞察。

---

## 15.2 System Role

## 15.2 系統角色

**EN**  
This combination provides facility managers and building operators with tools to monitor energy usage, optimize consumption, manage equipment, control automation, and report system status.

Artibird Hub provides local runtime orchestration. Artibird Gate enables device connectivity. Act / Act-E controls electrical loads. Sense provides environmental data. Sync coordinates appliances. Panel and App provide control interfaces. Vision and Edge provide visual and AI-assisted insights. Cloud supports reporting, historical analysis, and multi-site coordination.

**ZH-TW**  
此產品組合為設施管理人員與建築運營者提供工具，以監測能源使用、優化消耗、管理設備、控制自動化，並報告系統狀態。

Artibird Hub 提供本地 Runtime 編排。Artibird Gate 啟用設備連接。Act / Act-E 控制電力負載。Sense 提供環境數據。Sync 協同家電。Panel 與 App 提供控制介面。Vision 與 Edge 提供視覺與 AI 輔助洞察。Cloud 提供報告、歷史分析與多站點協同。

## 15.3 Core Functions

## 15.3 核心功能

**EN**  
This product combination provides actionable control and visibility for energy and facility management. Functions include:

- Electrical load monitoring and control through Act / Act-E.
    
- Environmental monitoring through Sense and Vision.
    
- Automation participation triggered by sensors, schedule, or runtime events.
    
- Multi-device scene execution for energy optimization.
    
- Historical and real-time reporting via App and Cloud.
    
- AI-assisted optimization through Edge where deployed.
    

**ZH-TW**  
此產品組合為能源與設施管理提供可執行控制與可視化。功能包括：

- 透過 Act / Act-E 進行電力負載監測與控制。
    
- 透過 Sense 與 Vision 進行環境監測。
    
- 由感測器、排程或 Runtime 事件觸發的自動化參與。
    
- 多設備場景執行以優化能源使用。
    
- 透過 App 與 Cloud 提供歷史與即時報告。
    
- 在已部署條件下，透過 Edge 提供 AI 輔助優化。

## 20.3 From Isolated Devices to Coordinated Systems

## 20.3 從孤立設備到協同系統

**EN**  
Many smart products operate as individual endpoints. A light switch controls a light. A sensor reports a value. A camera sends an alert. A plug turns power on or off. These products may be useful, but they often remain fragmented.

Artibird is designed around a different idea: products should participate in a coordinated system. A switch should not only switch. A sensor should not only measure. A plug should not only power on and off. A gateway should not only connect. Each product should contribute to runtime behavior, system state, user interaction, automation, sensing, execution, and visibility.

**ZH-TW**  
許多智慧產品仍以單一端點方式運作。開關控制一盞燈，感測器回報一個數值，攝影機發送一個警報，插座執行通電或斷電。這些產品可能有用，但常常彼此割裂。

Artibird 基於另一種理念設計：產品應該參與一個協同系統。開關不只是開關，感測器不只是量測，插座不只是通電與斷電，網關不只是連接。每個產品都應為 Runtime 行為、系統狀態、用戶互動、自動化、感知、執行與可視化提供貢獻。

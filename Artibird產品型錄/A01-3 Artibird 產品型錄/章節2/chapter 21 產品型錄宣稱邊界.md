# Chapter 21 — Product Catalog Claim Boundary

# 第二十一章｜產品型錄宣稱邊界

---

## 21.1 Purpose

## 21.1 目的

**EN**  
This chapter defines the boundaries for all claims presented in the Artibird Product Catalog. Its goal is to ensure that every feature, scenario, compatibility, energy, AI, security, privacy, and deployment statement is evidence-based, validated, and aligned with the approved SKU and product specifications.

It is intended to protect the brand, avoid overpromising, maintain trust with customers, and provide clear guidance to channel partners, sales teams, integrators, and marketing teams.

**ZH-TW**  
本章定義 Artibird 產品型錄中所有宣稱的邊界。其目標是確保每項功能、場景、相容性、能源、AI、安全、隱私與部署宣稱，都基於證據、經驗證，並與核准的 SKU 與產品規格書保持一致。

此章旨在保護品牌、避免過度承諾、維持客戶信任，並為渠道合作夥伴、銷售團隊、整合商及行銷團隊提供清晰指引。

---

## 21.2 Claim Governance Principle

## 21.2 宣稱治理原則

**EN**  
All catalog claims must be:

1. **Evidence-bound** — Linked to implementation, testing, validation, or certification.
    
2. **Condition-aware** — Qualified by model, firmware, hardware, region, deployment, and environmental conditions.
    
3. **Non-absolute** — Avoid language implying universal compatibility, guaranteed results, unrestricted automation, or full autonomy.
    
4. **System-aligned** — Ensure claims reflect the coordinated system behavior rather than individual device behavior alone.
    

**ZH-TW**  
所有型錄宣稱必須遵守：

1. **具證據性** — 與實作、測試、驗證或認證相關。
    
2. **條件感知** — 根據型號、韌體、硬體、區域、部署與環境條件加以限定。
    
3. **非絕對化** — 避免暗示通用相容、保證結果、不受限制自動化或完全自主的語言。
    
4. **系統一致性** — 確保宣稱反映協同系統行為，而非單一設備行為。

## 21.3 Claim Categories and Guidance

## 21.3 宣稱類別與指引

**EN**  
Claims in the catalog should be categorized and controlled according to product role, scenario, feature type, deployment, and supporting evidence.

**ZH-TW**  
型錄中的宣稱應依據產品角色、場景、功能類型、部署條件與支持證據進行分類與管控。

---

### English Table

|Claim Category|Description|Control Requirement|
|---|---|---|
|Product Role|Hub, Gate, Act, Sense, Switch, Panel, Sync, Vision, App|Must reflect system architecture and assigned runtime role|
|Feature|Device capability, automation, sensing, control|Must be validated and documented|
|Scenario|Deployment context (home, classroom, building, campus, energy, agriculture, infrastructure)|Must align with deployment validation|
|Compatibility|Interoperability with devices, protocols, cloud, or Edge|Must be verified under supported conditions|
|Energy|Energy-aware features, load monitoring, consumption reporting|Must be measured or validated; no guaranteed savings without evidence|
|AI / Edge|AI-assisted actions, visual or sensor interpretation|Must be bounded by deployment and validation|
|Security / Privacy|Access control, data handling, retention, compliance|Must reflect implemented functionality|
|Certification|UL, ETL, FCC, CE, RoHS|Must match completed certification records|
|Capacity / Limits|Max devices, concurrent events, throughput|Must be based on validated testing|
|Deployment|Site suitability, outdoor readiness, multi-zone|Must depend on survey, installation, and validation|

### 中文表

|宣稱類別|說明|管控要求|
|---|---|---|
|產品角色|Hub、Gate、Act、Sense、Switch、Panel、Sync、Vision、App|必須符合系統架構與分配 Runtime 角色|
|功能|設備能力、自動化、感知、控制|必須驗證並有文件記錄|
|場景|部署環境（住宅、教室、建築、校園、能源、農業、基礎設施）|必須符合部署驗證條件|
|相容性|與設備、協議、雲端或 Edge 的互操作性|必須在受支援條件下驗證|

|認證|UL、ETL、FCC、CE、RoHS|必須符合完成認證記錄|
|容量 / 限制|最大設備數、併發事件、吞吐量|必須基於已驗證測試|
|部署|場地適用性、戶外準備、多區域|必須依站點勘察、安裝與驗證|

## 21.4 Claim Approval Process

## 21.4 宣稱核准流程

**EN**  
Every catalog claim must pass through a controlled review process:

1. Draft claim with product mapping
    
2. Check implementation and validation evidence
    
3. Identify deployment and environmental conditions
    
4. Review for risk of overstatement
    
5. Align with system-level claim boundaries
    
6. Approve with product, engineering, compliance, legal, and marketing teams
    
7. Release and track version
    

**ZH-TW**  
每一項型錄宣稱必須經過受控審查流程：

1. 起草宣稱並進行產品映射
    
2. 檢查實作與驗證證據
    
3. 確認部署與環境條件
    
4. 審查過度宣稱風險
    
5. 與系統級宣稱邊界對齊
    
6. 經產品、工程、合規、法律與行銷團隊核准
    
7. 發布並追蹤版本

## 21.5 Chapter Conclusion

## 21.5 章節結論

**EN**  
The Product Catalog Claim Boundary chapter ensures that all customer-facing language is controlled, evidence-aware, condition-bound, and aligned with product specifications. It establishes trust, protects brand credibility, and provides a clear guideline for marketing, sales, channel, and deployment documentation.

**ZH-TW**  
產品型錄宣稱邊界章確保所有面向客戶的語言受到管控、具證據意識、受條件約束，並與產品規格書保持一致。它建立信任、保護品牌可信度，並為行銷、銷售、渠道與部署文件提供清晰指引。

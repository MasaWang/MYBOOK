# Chapter 19 — Product Claim Boundary

# 第十九章｜產品宣稱邊界

---

## 19.1 Purpose

## 19.1 目的

**EN**  
This chapter defines the product claim boundary for the Artibird Product Catalog. Its purpose is to ensure that all product descriptions, feature statements, scenario claims, compatibility statements, certification references, AI descriptions, security statements, privacy statements, energy statements, and deployment claims remain accurate, evidence-aware, and commercially responsible.

The Product Claim Boundary is especially important because Artibird products participate in the OceanAI coordinated intelligent environment system. Product capabilities may depend on model, firmware, hardware version, deployment conditions, cloud availability, Edge deployment, validation status, and certification status.

**ZH-TW**  
本章定義 Artibird 產品型錄中的產品宣稱邊界。其目的，是確保所有產品描述、功能聲明、場景宣稱、相容性描述、認證引用、AI 描述、安全聲明、隱私聲明、能源聲明與部署宣稱，都保持準確、具證據意識，並符合商業責任。

產品宣稱邊界對 Artibird 尤其重要，因為 Artibird 產品參與 OceanAI 協同智慧空間系統。產品能力可能取決於型號、韌體、硬體版本、部署條件、雲端可用性、Edge 部署、驗證狀態與認證狀態。

---

## 19.2 Claim Boundary Principle

## 19.2 宣稱邊界原則

**EN**  
Every Artibird product claim should follow one core principle:

**Do not claim beyond what is implemented, validated, certified, supported, and approved.**

Product catalog language should explain product value clearly, but it should not imply universal compatibility, guaranteed performance, unrestricted automation, full autonomy, completed certification, legal compliance, unlimited capacity, or guaranteed savings unless those claims are supported by evidence.

**ZH-TW**  
每一項 Artibird 產品宣稱都應遵循一項核心原則：

**不要宣稱超出已實作、已驗證、已認證、已支援與已核准的範圍。**

產品型錄語言應清楚說明產品價值，但不應暗示通用相容、保證性能、不受限制的自動化、完全自主、已完成認證、法律合規、無限制容量或保證節省，除非這些宣稱已有證據支持。

## 19.4 Approved Claim Language Pattern

## 19.4 建議宣稱語言模式

**EN**  
Artibird catalog claims should use structured, bounded language. The preferred pattern is:

**Product supports [defined capability] for [supported target] under [specific conditions].**

This structure allows product value to be communicated clearly while preserving accuracy.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird 型錄宣稱應使用結構化、受邊界約束的語言。建議句式為：

**產品在 [特定條件] 下，為 [受支援目標] 支援 [已定義能力]。**

這種結構能清楚傳達產品價值，同時保持準確性。

## 19.7 AI and Edge Claim Boundary

## 19.7 AI 與 Edge 宣稱邊界

**EN**  
AI and Edge-related language must be especially controlled. OceanAI Edge may support AI-assisted interpretation, visual context, sensor fusion, energy-aware recommendations, or optimization support where deployed and validated.

AI-related claims should not imply unrestricted autonomy, guaranteed decision accuracy, independent safety authority, or physical execution without runtime governance.

**ZH-TW**  
AI 與 Edge 相關語言必須特別受控。OceanAI Edge 可在已部署並驗證條件下，支援 AI 輔助解讀、視覺上下文、感測融合、能源感知建議或優化支持。

AI 相關宣稱不應暗示不受限制的自主性、保證決策準確、獨立安全權威，或不經 Runtime 治理直接進行物理執行。

## 19.8 Energy Claim Boundary

## 19.8 能源宣稱邊界

**EN**  
Energy-related claims should remain conservative and evidence-based. Artibird Act-E, Sense, Hub, Cloud, and Edge may support energy monitoring, energy-aware automation, reporting, alerts, and optimization workflows.

However, energy savings depend on baseline usage, building conditions, device behavior, user behavior, schedule configuration, climate, equipment efficiency, and validation method. The catalog should not guarantee savings unless measured under defined comparison conditions.

**ZH-TW**  
能源相關宣稱應保持保守並基於證據。Artibird Act-E、Sense、Hub、Cloud 與 Edge 可支援能源監測、能源感知自動化、報告、警報與優化工作流。

然而，節能效果取決於基準用電、建築條件、設備行為、用戶行為、排程配置、氣候、設備效率與驗證方法。除非已在明確比較條件下完成量測，型錄不應保證節能。

---

|---|---|
|Guaranteed energy savings|Supports energy-aware workflows where measured and validated|
|Automatically reduces energy bills|May help improve visibility and control of energy-related behavior|
|Saves 30% energy|Use only if supported by validated measurement and defined baseline|
|Optimizes all energy use|Supports selected energy monitoring, automation, and reporting workflows|
|Eliminates waste|Helps identify or reduce selected unnecessary equipment use where configured|
|AI maximizes energy efficiency|Edge may provide energy-aware recommendations where deployed and validated|

## 中文表

|避免使用|建議語言|
|---|---|

|節能 30%|僅在具備已驗證量測與明確基準時使用|

|消除浪費|在已配置條件下幫助識別或減少特定不必要設備使用|

## 19.9 Security and Privacy Claim Boundary

## 19.9 安全與隱私宣稱邊界

**EN**  
Security and privacy statements must reflect implemented features and actual data behavior. The catalog may describe access control, role permissions, privacy settings, local processing, cloud synchronization, Vision settings, and data retention only when those features are implemented and supported.

The catalog should not imply absolute security, legal compliance, no data risk, or complete privacy protection unless supported by formal review and evidence.

**ZH-TW**  
安全與隱私聲明必須反映已實作功能與實際數據行為。只有在相關功能已實作並受支援時，型錄才可描述存取控制、角色權限、隱私設定、本地處理、雲端同步、Vision 設定與數據保留。

型錄不應暗示絕對安全、法律合規、沒有數據風險或完整隱私保護，除非已有正式審查與證據支持。

---

|Avoid Saying|Preferred Language|

|Completely private|Supports defined privacy settings and data handling controls|
|No data leaves the home|Local processing is supported for defined functions; cloud features may synchronize selected data|
|Compliant with all privacy laws|Privacy compliance language requires legal review|
|Video is always private|Vision privacy depends on recording, access, storage, retention, and sharing settings|
|Hack-proof|Do not use; describe implemented security mechanisms instead|
|Users have total control of all data|Users may manage supported privacy and data settings where implemented|

## 中文表

|用戶完全控制所有數據|用戶可在已實作條件下管理受支援隱私與數據設定|

## 19.10 Certification Claim Boundary

## 19.10 認證宣稱邊界

**EN**  
Certification claims must be model-specific and market-specific. The catalog should not use certification language unless the certification has been completed for the exact product model, hardware version, configuration, region, and label scope.

Certification planning, test reports, component certifications, or supplier claims do not automatically equal final product certification.

**ZH-TW**  
認證宣稱必須是特定型號與特定市場的。除非特定產品型號、硬體版本、配置、區域與標籤範圍已完成認證，否則型錄不應使用認證語言。

認證規劃、測試報告、元件認證或供應商宣稱，不自動等同於最終產品認證。

---

|Avoid Saying|Preferred Language|

|ETL listed|Use only when exact model is listed and label use is approved|
|FCC approved|Use only when authorization is complete for the exact model|
|CE compliant|Use only when applicable documentation and conformity process are complete|
|Certified components mean certified product|Component certification does not automatically certify the finished product|
|Safety certified|Use model-specific certification status only|
|Ready for all markets|Market readiness depends on region-specific certification and labeling|

## 中文表

|避免使用|建議語言|

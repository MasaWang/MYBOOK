# 2. Artibird Product Line Definition

# 2. Artibird 產品線定義

## EN

Artibird is the product layer of the OceanAI coordinated intelligence architecture. While OceanAI defines the system-level intelligence architecture, Artibird represents the physical and interactive product family that brings this architecture into real spaces.

The Artibird product line is designed around coordinated intelligent environments. Instead of treating each device as an isolated smart product, Artibird organizes devices into a runtime ecosystem where connection, sensing, interaction, execution, synchronization, visual awareness, and local coordination work together.

## ZH-TW

Artibird 是 OceanAI 協同智慧架構中的產品層。OceanAI 定義系統級智慧架構，而 Artibird 則代表將這一架構帶入真實空間的實體與互動產品家族。

Artibird 產品線圍繞協同智慧空間而設計。它不是將每個設備視為孤立智慧產品，而是將設備組織為一個 Runtime 生態，使連接、感知、互動、執行、同步、視覺感知與本地協同共同運作。

# 4. OceanAI and Artibird Relationship

# 4. OceanAI 與 Artibird 的關係

## EN

OceanAI and Artibird should be understood as different layers of the same intelligent environment strategy.

OceanAI is the system-level intelligence architecture. It defines coordination, runtime behavior, local-first logic, cloud enhancement, edge intelligence, data flow, and system governance.

Artibird is the product family that makes this architecture visible and usable. It includes the devices, interfaces, gateways, hubs, execution nodes, sensors, panels, switches, vision nodes, and synchronization products that operate inside real spaces.

## ZH-TW

OceanAI 與 Artibird 應被理解為同一智慧空間策略中的不同層級。

OceanAI 是系統級智慧架構，定義協同、Runtime 行為、本地優先邏輯、雲端增強、邊緣智慧、數據流與系統治理。

Artibird 是讓這一架構變得可見、可使用的產品家族。它包含在真實空間中運作的設備、介面、網關、中樞、執行節點、感測器、面板、開關、視覺節點與同步產品。

# 6. Product Line Architecture

# 6. 產品線架構

## EN

The Artibird product line can be understood through eight functional layers:

1. **Runtime Coordination** — Artibird Hub
    
2. **Device Access** — Artibird Gate
    
3. **Appliance Synchronization** — Artibird Sync
    
4. **Physical Interaction** — Artibird Switch
    
5. **Room Interface** — Artibird Panel
    
6. **Electrical Execution** — Artibird Act
    
7. **Environmental Context** — Artibird Sense
    
8. **Visual Awareness** — Artibird Vision
    

Together, these layers allow intelligent spaces to sense conditions, receive user intention, coordinate system logic, execute physical actions, and extend management through cloud and edge capabilities.

## ZH-TW

Artibird 產品線可透過八個功能層級理解：

1. **Runtime 協同** — Artibird Hub
    
2. **設備接入** — Artibird Gate
    
3. **家電同步** — Artibird Sync
    
4. **實體互動** — Artibird Switch
    
5. **房間介面** — Artibird Panel
    
6. **電力執行** — Artibird Act
    
7. **環境上下文** — Artibird Sense
    
8. **視覺感知** — Artibird Vision
    

這些層級共同使智慧空間能夠感知條件、接收用戶意圖、協同系統邏輯、執行物理動作，並透過雲端與邊緣能力擴展管理。

# 8. Product Category Summaries

# 8. 產品分類摘要

## 8.1 Artibird Hub

## 8.1 Artibird Hub

**EN**  
Artibird Hub is the local runtime center of the Artibird ecosystem. It hosts or supports local services such as device state management, local messaging, automation logic, data flow, and cloud synchronization. Hub is the practical operating center that allows supported Artibird devices to behave as one coordinated local system.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird Hub 是 Artibird 生態中的本地 Runtime 中心。它承載或支援設備狀態管理、本地消息、自動化邏輯、數據流與雲端同步等本地服務。Hub 是使受支援 Artibird 設備作為一個協同本地系統運作的實際運作中心。

---

## 8.2 Artibird Gate

## 8.2 Artibird Gate

**EN**  
Artibird Gate is the localized network gateway and device access node. It helps supported Artibird devices join the local runtime system and participate in coordinated behavior. Gate is especially important for device onboarding, local network participation, gateway communication, and capacity-aware device access.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird Gate 是本地化網路網關與設備接入節點。它幫助受支援 Artibird 設備加入本地 Runtime 系統並參與協同行為。Gate 對設備入網、本地網路參與、網關通訊與容量感知設備接入尤其重要。

---

## 8.3 Artibird Sync

## 8.3 Artibird Sync

**EN**  
Artibird Sync is the IR / RF synchronization node. It allows supported infrared and selected RF appliances to participate in scenes, automation, and coordinated system behavior. Sync is valuable for spaces that need to bring existing appliances such as air conditioners, TVs, projectors, fans, and selected RF devices into intelligent operation without replacing them.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird Sync 是 IR / RF 同步節點。它使受支援紅外線與部分 RF 家電能夠參與場景、自動化與協同系統行為。Sync 適合需要在不替換設備的情況下，將既有空調、電視、投影機、風扇與部分 RF 設備納入智慧運作的空間。

---

## 8.4 Artibird Switch

## 8.4 Artibird Switch

**EN**  
Artibird Switch is the smart switch and event interaction product family. Depending on product variant, it may support relay-based load control, scene triggering, battery-powered event transmission, local automation, and remote visibility. Switch turns physical touch into system-level behavior.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird Switch 是智慧開關與事件互動產品家族。依產品版本不同，它可支援繼電器負載控制、場景觸發、電池事件發射、本地自動化與遠端可視化。Switch 將實體觸控轉化為系統級行為。

---

## 8.5 Artibird Panel

## 8.5 Artibird Panel

**EN**  
Artibird Panel is the room-level control interface of the Artibird ecosystem. It provides a visible and shared touch interface for scenes, device groups, room modes, environmental status, and local runtime actions. Panel is useful for classrooms, meeting rooms, hotel rooms, offices, and shared spaces.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird Panel 是 Artibird 生態中的房間級控制介面。它為場景、設備群組、房間模式、環境狀態與本地 Runtime 動作提供可見且可共享的觸控介面。Panel 適用於教室、會議室、酒店房間、辦公室與共享空間。

---

## 8.6 Artibird Act

## 8.6 Artibird Act

**EN**  
Artibird Act is the electrical execution node of the product family. It turns system decisions into controlled electrical actions through supported smart plug, relay, in-box, or energy-enabled variants. Act allows the system to execute lighting, appliance, power, energy, and load-control workflows.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird Act 是產品家族中的電力執行節點。它透過受支援智慧插座、繼電器、底盒內或能源版本，將系統決策轉化為受控電力動作。Act 使系統能夠執行照明、家電、電源、能源與負載控制工作流。

---

## 8.7 Artibird Sense

## 8.7 Artibird Sense

**EN**  
Artibird Sense is the environmental sensing node of the Artibird ecosystem. It provides environmental context such as temperature, humidity, light, occupancy, air, water, soil, or other sensor data depending on product variant. Sense allows automation and system response to be informed by real environmental conditions.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird Sense 是 Artibird 生態中的環境感知節點。依產品版本不同，它可提供溫度、濕度、光照、人在、空氣、水、土壤或其他感測數據。Sense 使自動化與系統回應能夠基於真實環境條件。

---

## 8.8 Artibird Vision

## 8.8 Artibird Vision

**EN**  
Artibird Vision is the visual context and AI vision product family. It provides visual awareness, security context, motion or event detection, local or cloud recording workflows, and OceanAI Edge-assisted visual intelligence where supported. Vision allows the system to understand visual events and respond through coordinated behavior.

**ZH-TW**  
Artibird Vision 是視覺上下文與 AI 視覺產品家族。它在支援條件下提供視覺感知、安全上下文、移動或事件偵測、本地或雲端錄影工作流，以及 OceanAI Edge 輔助視覺智慧。Vision 使系統能理解視覺事件並透過協同行為作出回應。

# 11. Product Line Value

# 11. 產品線價值

## EN

The value of Artibird is not only in individual devices, but in how those devices work together. Hub coordinates, Gate connects, Sense provides context, Switch and Panel capture intention, Act executes electrical action, Sync brings legacy appliances into the system, and Vision provides visual awareness.

Together, these products allow spaces to move from isolated device control to coordinated system behavior.

## ZH-TW

Artibird 的價值不只在單一設備，而在於這些設備如何共同運作。Hub 負責協同，Gate 負責接入，Sense 提供上下文，Switch 與 Panel 捕捉意圖，Act 執行電力動作，Sync 將傳統家電納入系統，Vision 提供視覺感知。

這些產品共同使空間從孤立設備控制走向協同系統行為。

# 12. Commercial Boundaries

# 12. 商業邊界

## EN

Artibird products should be presented with clear commercial boundaries. Product claims must be based on supported product variants, validated conditions, compatibility matrices, certification status, and deployment requirements.

The product line should avoid absolute claims such as unlimited capacity, universal compatibility, guaranteed energy savings, perfect AI detection, full offline operation for all functions, or compatibility with all wiring and all appliances.

## ZH-TW

Artibird 產品應以清晰商業邊界呈現。產品宣稱必須基於受支援產品版本、已驗證條件、相容性矩陣、認證狀態與部署要求。

產品線應避免使用無限制容量、通用相容、保證節能、完美 AI 偵測、所有功能完全離線、適用所有接線與所有家電等絕對化宣稱。

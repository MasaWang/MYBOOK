# 3. Product Commercial Overview

# 3. 產品商業總覽

## EN

Artibird Act is the electrical execution product family within the Artibird ecosystem. It provides controlled electrical action for supported loads, plugs, relays, circuits, and equipment, allowing the Artibird system to move from sensing and decision-making into physical execution.

The Artibird Act family may include smart plug variants, relay-control variants, in-box execution nodes, distribution-box variants, and energy-measurement variants. Standard Act models focus on controlled on/off execution. Energy-enabled models, such as Act-E variants, may provide voltage, current, power, energy-consumption, and load-state data where implemented and validated.

In homes, classrooms, offices, campuses, commercial buildings, and infrastructure deployments, Artibird Act can support lighting control, appliance power control, classroom energy management, HVAC-related workflows, plug-level monitoring, and load-based automation. When paired with Artibird Hub, ArtiOS, Switch, Panel, Sense, Sync, Vision, OceanAI Cloud, and OceanAI Edge, Act becomes part of a coordinated runtime system where electrical execution responds to scenes, schedules, sensor events, user actions, and system policies.

Artibird Act follows a local-first, cloud-enhanced model. Supported local relay actions, plug control, and automation execution can operate within the local environment when required system components are present. Cloud services may enhance the system with remote control, energy reports, lifecycle management, alerts, analytics, and multi-site visibility.

Artibird Act should not be presented as a universal load-control device. Load rating, safety behavior, relay capacity, wiring method, plug type, measurement accuracy, thermal performance, and regional certification must be defined by product variant and validated under specified conditions.

## ZH-TW

Artibird Act 是 Artibird 生態中的電力執行產品家族。它為受支援負載、插座、繼電器、迴路與設備提供受控電力動作，使 Artibird 系統能夠從感知與決策進入實體執行。

Artibird Act 家族可包含智慧插座版本、繼電器控制版本、底盒內執行節點、配電箱版本與電能計量版本。標準 Act 型號主要聚焦受控開 / 關執行。帶能源能力的版本，例如 Act-E，可在已實作並驗證條件下提供電壓、電流、功率、用電量與負載狀態數據。

在住宅、教室、辦公室、校園、商業建築與基礎設施部署中，Artibird Act 可支援照明控制、家電電源控制、教室能源管理、HVAC 相關工作流、插座級監測與基於負載的自動化。當它與 Artibird Hub、ArtiOS、Switch、Panel、Sense、Sync、Vision、OceanAI Cloud 和 OceanAI Edge 配合使用時，Act 成為協同 Runtime 系統的一部分，使電力執行能根據場景、排程、感測事件、用戶動作與系統策略作出回應。

Artibird Act 採用本地優先、雲端增強模式。在所需系統元件具備的條件下，受支援本地繼電器動作、插座控制與自動化執行可在本地環境中運作。雲端服務可進一步透過遠端控制、能源報告、生命週期管理、警報、分析與多站點可視化增強系統能力。

Artibird Act 不應被表述為通用負載控制設備。負載額定、安全行為、繼電器容量、接線方式、插頭類型、計量精度、熱性能與地區認證，必須依產品版本定義並在指定條件下完成驗證。

# 12. Commercial Claim Policy

# 12. 商業宣稱政策

## EN

All Artibird Act commercial claims must remain consistent with the related engineering specification and validation results. Commercial materials must not use absolute claims such as “controls all loads,” “supports all appliances,” “guaranteed energy savings,” “universal relay,” “safe for every circuit,” or “certified safe” unless these claims are supported by completed certification and clearly defined validation evidence.

Act-related claims must distinguish between plug variants, relay variants, in-box variants, distribution-box variants, and energy-measurement variants. Energy-related claims must distinguish between measurement capability, monitoring value, automation support, and actual energy-saving results. Energy savings should not be promised unless supported by measured data under defined use cases.

Electrical and safety claims must be tied to voltage, current, load type, wiring method, thermal behavior, enclosure design, regional standards, certification status, and validation reports.

## ZH-TW

所有 Artibird Act 商業宣稱必須與對應工程規格書與驗證結果保持一致。商業資料不得使用「控制所有負載」「支援所有家電」「保證節能」「通用繼電器」「適用所有電路」「已認證安全」等絕對化宣稱，除非這些宣稱已有完整認證與明確驗證證據支持。

Act 相關宣稱必須區分插座版本、繼電器版本、底盒內版本、配電箱版本與電能計量版本。能源相關宣稱必須區分計量能力、監測價值、自動化支援與實際節能結果。除非具備明確使用場景下的實測數據，否則不得承諾節能效果。

電氣與安全宣稱必須綁定電壓、電流、負載類型、接線方式、熱行為、外殼設計、地區標準、認證狀態與驗證報告。
